About the Band

We Are Not Your Kind

Knot Your Kind delivers the most intense and authentic Slipknot tribute experience in the Southeast. From the masks to the jumpsuits, from the crushing riffs to the primal screams — every detail is crafted to honor the legacy of the nine.

Performing across the Carolinas and beyond, we bring the full chaos and energy of a Slipknot show to every stage we hit. Nine members. Nine masks. One mission: to give fans the closest thing to a real Slipknot experience.

Booked through Left Lane Collective, we are available for festivals, venues, private events, and everything in between.
